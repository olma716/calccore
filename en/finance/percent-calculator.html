FAQ

How do I calculate X% of a number?

Multiply the number by the percent and divide by 100. Example: 20% of 500 = 500 × 20 / 100 = 100.

How to find how many percent one number is of another?

Divide the part by the total and multiply by 100. This is commonly used to compare prices, growth, and ratios.

How does increase by percent work?

Add the percentage of the number to itself. Example: add 15% to 200 → 200 + (200 × 15 / 100) = 230.

How to calculate a discount in percent?

A discount is a percent of the original price. Example: 25% off 800 means you save 200 and pay 600.

Can I use it for finance calculations?
