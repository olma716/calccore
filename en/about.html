About CalcCore

CalcCore is a set of clear calculators for quick everyday planning: from car import costs to loans, payments and schedules.

Why CalcCore

We build calculators that provide a fast and understandable estimate and show the breakdown of the result. The goal is to help you plan a budget, compare options and make decisions with transparent numbers.

No registration, no “magic”, no clutter — just what you need for the calculation.

How calculators work

Each calculator uses financial formulas or simplified models to deliver a quick estimate. Where the outcome depends on external factors (customs valuation, exemptions, bank policies, fees), we try to highlight that in hints and explanations.

Typical flow: enter inputs → get totals → see the breakdown.

Data sources

We use reliable sources when external data is required. For example:

• FX rates (USD/EUR → UAH) — official National Bank of Ukraine (NBU) rates (may be cached in the browser per day).
• Loan math — standard financial formulas (annuity / decreasing payments).

If you want, message us which parameters or sources should be added/clarified for a specific calculator.

Privacy

Inputs you enter are used for the calculation on the page. In some cases, local preferences may be stored (e.g., rate cache) in your browser.

The site uses analytics (Google tag) to improve the product.

Accuracy & disclaimer

CalcCore results are estimates intended for planning and comparison. Real numbers may differ due to official rounding rules, bank/service policies, fees, documents, exemptions and other conditions.

CalcCore is not financial or legal advice. Always verify final terms with official sources/professionals.
