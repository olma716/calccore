Total cost of a car from Europe

This calculator helps you quickly estimate the total ‚Äúturnkey‚Äù cost of importing a car from Europe to Ukraine. You immediately see the realistic amount, including customs payments and all additional expenses.

You can enter the car price in euros, dollars or hryvnias. The calculator converts the amount using current NBU rates and uses it to estimate duty, excise and VAT.

The calculator includes:

  • car price (any currency)
  • delivery to Ukraine
  • duty, excise and VAT
  • certification and registration
  • first service, agent fee and contingency

The result is an estimate suitable for budgeting, comparing cars and planning before purchase.

FAQ

Can I enter the car price in UAH or USD?

Yes. You can enter the car price in UAH, USD or EUR. The calculator converts the amount using NBU rates.

How accurate is the customs estimate?

This is an estimate. Real totals may vary depending on customs valuation, documents, exemptions and vehicle category.

Is delivery included?

Yes. Delivery is added to the customs value and affects duty and VAT.

Does it work for electric cars?

Yes. For EVs the calculator uses a simplified model, where duty and/or excise may be reduced or zero depending on the rules.

What is this calculator best for?

Budget planning, comparing different cars, and understanding the real import cost before buying.
