FAQ about car running costs

How much does 100 km cost on gasoline?

It depends on fuel consumption and price per liter. For example, 8 L/100 km at 55 UAH/L costs 440 UAH per 100 km.

How much does 1 km cost in an electric car?

If an EV uses 18 kWh per 100 km and electricity costs 6 UAH per kWh, then 1 km costs about 1.08 UAH (before losses).

What’s cheaper — an EV or a gasoline car?

In most cases an EV is cheaper to run, especially with frequent driving and home charging. This calculator helps compare EV, hybrid and ICE precisely.

How do I calculate trip expenses?

Enter trip distance, fuel or electricity consumption and price. The calculator shows total cost, cost per 1 km and per 100 km.

Can I compare two cars?

Yes. Switch to “2 cars” mode and compare any two cars — diesel vs hybrid or EV vs gasoline — to see which is cheaper to operate.
