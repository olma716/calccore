How the car customs calculator works

This customs calculator helps you quickly estimate import payments when bringing a car to Ukraine from Europe or the USA: duty, excise and VAT.

Enter the car price, delivery, year of manufacture and engine parameters — the calculator automatically determines the customs value and totals in UAH.

For electric vehicles, duty is often 0%, while excise is calculated from battery capacity (kWh).

VAT when importing a car to Ukraine: what to know

VAT on car import is not charged only on the “car price”. The VAT base usually includes the customs value (price + delivery/insurance to border) plus duty and excise.

In this calculator VAT is calculated as: (customs value + duty + excise) × VAT rate.

Excise in Ukraine: what it depends on

Excise is a separate payment. For ICE vehicles (simplified model) it depends on fuel type, engine size and vehicle age.

For electric vehicles excise is: 1€ × kWh.

FAQ about car customs clearance

What is included in customs clearance cost?

Duty, excise and VAT. The calculator shows a breakdown for each payment.

Is delivery included?

Yes. Delivery/insurance is added to the car price and forms the customs value.

How is excise calculated?

For ICE — depends on fuel type, engine size and age. For EV — 1€ × kWh.

How accurate is this calculator?

This is an MVP version for quick estimation. For broker-level accuracy, more parameters are needed.
